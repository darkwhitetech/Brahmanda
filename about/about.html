Our Philosophy

At Bramhanda, we believe that music is the universal language that connects souls. Our studio was born from a passion for pristine sound and a desire to create an environment where artists can truly flourish. We blend state-of-the-art technology with a deep understanding of musical artistry to help you achieve the perfect sound. Whether you are a seasoned professional or a budding artist, our mission is to provide you with the tools, expertise, and creative space to bring your sonic vision to life. We are more than just a studio; we are collaborators in your creative journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything about our music studio

What recording equipment do you have? ▼

Our studio is equipped with industry-standard gear including SSL console, Neumann and AKG microphones, Universal Audio preamps, Pro Tools HDX system, and a collection of vintage and modern outboard gear. We also have a wide selection of instruments including guitars, keyboards, and drums.

How much does studio time cost? ▼

Our hourly rates vary depending on the engineer level and services required. Standard recording sessions start at competitive rates, with discounted packages available for full-day bookings and multi-day projects. Contact us for a custom quote tailored to your project needs.

Do you offer mixing and mastering services? ▼

Yes! We provide professional mixing and mastering services for all genres. Our experienced engineers use a hybrid approach combining analog warmth with digital precision. We can work with stems recorded at our studio or external recordings, and offer revision rounds to ensure your satisfaction.

Can I bring my own producer or engineer? ▼

Absolutely! You're welcome to bring your own producer or engineer. We offer dry hire rates for independent sessions, and our staff is available to assist with technical setup and ensure everything runs smoothly. We're here to support your creative vision however works best for you.
